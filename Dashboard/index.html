More about the data

This dashboard presents data from the top 200 most streamed songs on Spotify from January 2017 to June 2019, the streams count does not represent the actual "streams" of a song but the streams it had while the track was part of the top 200 most streamed Songs. We can see that Shape of You by Ed Sheeran has more than 59 million streams while the song was on the top 200, but actually the song reached over 2.8 billion streams in total.
